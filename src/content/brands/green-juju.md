## About

Green Juju isn't a complete vegan diet — it's a **fresh vegetable topper**
designed to be added on top of any base food. The blends are raw, frozen, and
formulated to add plant nutrients (chlorophyll, fiber, antioxidants) that are
hard to get into kibble-only diets.

## Product range

- **Original Green Juju** — kale, parsley, celery, ginger
- **Goji Blend** — antioxidant-focused
- **Beet Blend** — circulation and recovery

## Strengths

- Strong independent retailer network in US
- High-quality whole-food ingredients
- Useful for dogs on hypoallergenic diets

## Caveats

- Frozen — requires freezer space and ships in US-only cold chain
- Topper, not a complete diet
