## About

V-Planet is the **Asia-Pacific sister brand of V-dog** (San Francisco, family-owned since 2005). Same parent company, same nutritional philosophy, different regional supply chain — V-Planet is the kibble line specifically distributed across APAC through regional partners.

For APAC pet parents, this matters enormously: V-dog's flagship US product is hard to import (no direct shipping), but V-Planet is **stocked locally** in Japan, Korea, Hong Kong, Singapore, Thailand, Australia, and New Zealand.

## Why APAC pet parents care

This is **the most APAC-accessible AAFCO-complete vegan dog kibble** on the market. While most US/UK vegan brands require forwarders and 2–3 week shipping, V-Planet has established local distributors in 7+ APAC countries with normal retail-pace delivery.

## Product range (vegan)

- **V-Planet Original Kibble** — for medium/large dogs (peas, lentils, quinoa, brown rice base)
- **V-Planet Mini Bites** — smaller pieces formulated for small breeds

24% plant-powered protein. Free from animal products, corn, wheat, and soy.

## Strengths

- **20-year operating track record** via V-dog parent company
- AAFCO-complete (legal "complete diet" status, not just supplement)
- **Strongest APAC distribution** of any vegan dog food brand
- Vet + canine nutritionist formulated
- Excludes top allergens (no corn, wheat, soy in addition to no animal products)
- Made in Canada (high regulatory standards)

## Caveats

- **Adult dogs only** — not formulated for puppies, pregnant/nursing dogs, or seniors with specific needs (consult a vet for life-stage transitions)
- **Price-point is premium** — expect SGD $40–60 for a mid-size bag (varies by market and distributor)
- **No wet food option** (kibble-only line in APAC)
- Same parent as V-dog (already in this directory) — included separately because **V-Planet's APAC SKUs and distribution are genuinely different** from V-dog's US lineup
