## About

Yarrah is a Dutch B-Corp founded in 1992, making it one of the oldest organic
pet food brands in Europe. Their **vegetarian dog food line** is EU Organic
certified and Vegan Society approved.

## Why APAC pet parents care

Yarrah is one of the few certified organic vegan dog foods that **actually
ships to Hong Kong** through specialty importers. For HK pet parents wanting
European-grade organic certification, this is currently the most accessible
option.

## Product range (vegan/vegetarian)

- **Yarrah Organic Vegetarian Adult Dog** — wheat + soy protein
- **Vegetarian Wet Food** — chickpea + carrot

## Where to buy in APAC

- **Hong Kong**: Whiskers N Paws, Pet Pet Park (specialty importers)
- **Singapore**: limited via specialty boutiques
- **Taiwan**: not currently distributed (use forwarder)

## Strengths

- 30+ years of organic operating history
- Triple certification (EU Organic + Vegan Society + B-Corp)
- Wet food option (rare in vegan range)

## Caveats

- Premium pricing (~HK$280/3kg)
- HK distribution only — TW/SG buyers need forwarders
