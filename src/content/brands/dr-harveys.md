## About

Dr. Harvey's takes a different approach: rather than selling a complete meal,
they sell a **dehydrated vegetable + grain base** that pet parents reconstitute
with water and supplement themselves. **Veg-to-Bowl** is the line that, when
combined with vegan supplements (B12, taurine, omegas), becomes a complete
plant-based meal.
