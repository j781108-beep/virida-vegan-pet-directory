## About

Beethoven & Co is a Singapore-based startup making vegan pet treats targeting
the local SEA market. Founded in 2022, they're one of the few **homegrown SEA
vegan pet brands** — most competitors in the region are imports.

## Why this matters for APAC

Local SEA brands have key advantages:
- **No import duties** in regional markets
- **Climate-appropriate packaging** (tropical humidity tolerance)
- **Local customer support**
- Faster shipping

## Where to buy

- **Singapore**: direct site + Pets' Station selected branches
- **Malaysia**: direct shipping + Lazada
