## About

Petaluma is a newer US vegan dog food brand (founded 2020) focused on
**whole-food, recognizable ingredients** rather than isolated proteins. Their
formulation lead is a board-certified veterinary nutritionist.

## Why mention in APAC directory

While Petaluma isn't yet in APAC, it represents the **next-gen direction** of
vegan pet food (whole-food, vet-formulated, transparency). It's a useful
benchmark for what APAC pet parents should expect from imported brands.

## Strengths

- Vet nutritionist on the formulation team
- Whole-food ingredient story (no protein isolates)
- Transparent ingredient sourcing

## Caveats

- US-only, no APAC distribution
- Premium pricing
