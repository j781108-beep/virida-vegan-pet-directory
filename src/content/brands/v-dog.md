## About

V-dog is one of the longest-running vegan dog food brands in North America,
founded in 2005 in San Francisco. The company is family-owned and was an early
proof that complete plant-based dog nutrition was viable.

## Product range

- **Kind Kibble** — flagship AAFCO-complete dry food (the original)
- **Wheat-Free Kibble** — for sensitive dogs
- **Breath Bones** — dental treats
- **Supplements** — flax-based omega blend

## Strengths

- 20+ years of operating data — strong long-term feeding records
- AAFCO-complete certified
- Lower price than Wild Earth, similar nutrition profile
- Direct subscription with discounts

## Caveats

- US-only shipping
- Less marketing presence than Wild Earth, slower brand recognition outside the vegan community
