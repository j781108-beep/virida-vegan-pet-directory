## About

Edgard & Cooper is a Belgian B-Corp pet food brand best known for fresh meat
kibble — but they also produce a fully vegan line for plant-based households.
The brand has strong design, sustainability credentials, and EU-wide
distribution.

## Vegan range

- **Plant-Based Adult Kibble** — pea + sunflower protein
- **Plant-Based Treats** — banana + carob

## Strengths

- B-Corp certified (rare for pet food)
- Carbon-neutral shipping in UK
- Compostable kibble bags
- Strong retail presence in EU specialty stores

## Caveats

- Vegan range is smaller than meat range (positioning is "we offer vegan, but it's not our core")
- More expensive than US vegan options
