## About

Lily's Kitchen is one of the most recognized premium pet food brands in the UK
(acquired by Nestlé Purina in 2020). Their **vegan range** is Vegan Society
certified and gives pet parents access to plant-based diets through
mainstream retail channels rather than niche specialty shops.

## Vegan products

- **Plant Power Adult Dry** — pea protein kibble
- **Veggie Stew Wet Food** — wet pouches with vegetables
- **Meat-Free Multipack** — variety bundle

## Where to buy in APAC

- **Hong Kong**: Whiskers N Paws (limited SKUs)
- **Singapore**: special order only
- **Taiwan**: not currently distributed

## Strengths

- Major brand backing = supply chain reliability
- Wet food option (rare for vegan pet)
- Strong brand recognition for gifting

## Caveats

- Vegan range is a small portion of catalog — risk of discontinuation
- Less plant-based mission alignment vs Wild Earth/V-dog/VIRIDA
- Limited APAC availability
