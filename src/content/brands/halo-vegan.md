## About

Halo Holistic is a US pet food brand operating since 1986. Their **Vegan
Garden Medley** is one of the most globally accessible plant-based dog foods
because Halo sells through iHerb, Amazon Global, and major Asian retailers.

- **Most globally accessible** vegan dog food via iHerb
