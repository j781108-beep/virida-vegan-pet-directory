## About

Beco Pets is the **vegan/eco standard** for pet accessories — bowls, beds,
toys, leads, and waste bags. Their flagship rice-husk bowl was an industry
first when launched in 2009. The brand is B-Corp certified and PETA-approved
vegan across all materials.

## Product range

- **Bamboo + rice-husk bowls**
- **Recycled rubber toys** (squeak + tug)
- **Bamboo fiber leads & collars**
- **Compostable poop bags**
- **Hemp dog beds**

## Where to buy in APAC

- **Singapore**: Pet Lovers Centre, Pets' Station
- **Hong Kong**: Whiskers N Paws
- **Taiwan**: limited via independent importers
- **Malaysia**: online via Lazada specialty sellers

## Strengths

- Most accessible vegan pet **accessories** brand in APAC
- B-Corp certification
- Wide retail distribution
- Reasonable price ($)

## Caveats

- Not food/treats — purely accessories category
- Bamboo bowls can crack if dropped (less durable than ceramic)
