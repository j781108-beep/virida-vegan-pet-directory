## About

Vegan cat food is **one of the most controversial topics in pet nutrition** —
cats are obligate carnivores requiring taurine, arachidonic acid, and certain
amino acids historically only found in animal tissue. Ami Cat solves this with
**synthetically supplemented** versions of all critical nutrients.

## Important veterinary note

Before switching a cat to vegan food, consult a vet and **monitor urinary pH**.
Vegan cat diets can cause urinary alkalinity, which raises crystal/stone risk.
Ami includes urinary acidifiers but regular vet checks are non-negotiable.
