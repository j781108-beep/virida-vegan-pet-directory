## About

Wild Earth pioneered yeast-protein-based vegan dog food in the US. Their
flagship dry kibble is AAFCO-complete and balanced for adult dogs, using
*koji*-fermented yeast as the primary protein source. The brand received an
investment from Mark Cuban on *Shark Tank* and has scaled significantly.

## Product range

- **Performance Dog Food** — high-protein dry kibble (kibble form)
- **Maintenance Dog Food** — adult-formula vegan kibble
- **Treats** — peanut butter, banana, koji-yeast biscuits
- **Supplements** — skin & coat oil

## Strengths

- AAFCO-complete (legally a "complete diet", not a supplement)
- Strong veterinary advisory board
- Subscription-friendly pricing in US

## Caveats

- Currently US/Canada only — international shipping limited
- Higher price point ($3–4/lb)
