## About

Bonza is an Australian vegan dog food startup that distinguishes itself
through native Australian botanical ingredients. Founded in 2020 and based in
Sydney, the brand focuses on the Australian and New Zealand markets via DTC
subscription.

## Product range

- **Bonza Dry Food** — pea + wheat protein, fortified with native botanicals
- **Boost** — daily supplement chew

## Strengths

- Premium positioning — strong brand design and storytelling
- Native ingredient story (kakadu plum has 60× the vitamin C of oranges)
- Carbon-offset shipping in AU

## Caveats

- AU/NZ only at the moment
- Pricing is at the high end (~AUD $90/month for medium dog)
