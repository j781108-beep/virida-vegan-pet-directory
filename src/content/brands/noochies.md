## About

Noochies! is a small NYC startup making **single-ingredient training treats**
from puffed nutritional yeast (the same "nooch" used in vegan parmesan
cheese). The savory umami flavor is highly palatable to dogs, and the single-
ingredient approach is ideal for elimination diets and allergy testing.

## Product range

- **Original Noochies! Puffs** — pure nutritional yeast
- **Pumpkin Noochies!** — with pumpkin powder

## Strengths

- 1 ingredient — easiest possible label to read
- Excellent for training (small size, high reward value)
- Naturally rich in B-vitamins
- Affordable (~$8/bag)

## Caveats

- Treat-only, not for primary nutrition
- US-only shipping currently
