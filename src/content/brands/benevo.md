## About

Benevo is a UK-based pioneer in vegan pet food, founded in 2005. They are one
of the few brands offering a **vegan cat food** that meets nutritional
adequacy standards (cats are obligate carnivores, so this is a much harder
problem than dog food).

## Product range

- **Benevo Original** — dry dog food
- **Benevo Duo** — for dogs and cats both
- **Benevo Cat** — taurine-supplemented vegan cat food
- **Benevo Active** — high-energy formula
- **Wet food** — canned options for dogs and cats

## Strengths

- Vegan Society certified (UK gold-standard certification)
- Long European track record
- Multi-species range (rare in vegan pet food)

## Caveats

- Vegan cat food remains debated in the veterinary community — discuss with a vet before switching cats
